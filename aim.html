AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

Established in the year 1987,the school is owned and managed by the mavelikara taulk unit of Sree Narayana Samskarika Samithy; a charitable organization .

Sree Narayana Guru,the exponent who inspired to dispel social evils from the society serves as the light and guidance of this institution.

The school aims at instilling values to gain strength through solidarity and organisation and to get enlightenment through education.
